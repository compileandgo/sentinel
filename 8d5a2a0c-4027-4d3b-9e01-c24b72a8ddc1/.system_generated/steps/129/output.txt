With 100 tenants of 1 GB each, querying one tenant's namespace costs 1 RU. Using metadata filtering in a single 100 GB namespace would cost 100 RUs for the same query, because it scans all data regardless of filters.\n\n  * **Simple tenant offboarding:** To offboard a tenant/customer, you just [delete the relevant namespace](/guides/manage-data/delete-data#delete-all-records-from-a-namespace).
